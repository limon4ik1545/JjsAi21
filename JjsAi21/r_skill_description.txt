R-Skill Description: Player Swap
================================

Name: R - Position Swap (Camera Direction Swap)

Type: Utility / Tactical Movement

Description:
This skill instantly swaps the positions of the user and the target player. 
When activated, both players exchange their current coordinates on the map.

Key Mechanics:
1. Position Exchange:
   - User moves to target's location.
   - Target moves to user's previous location.
   - Distance between players does not affect activation (global range).

2. Camera Direction:
   - The camera direction also swaps relative to the new positions.
   - After the swap, the user's view aligns with the direction the target was facing.
   - This can be used to disorient opponents or quickly reposition for an attack.

3. Cooldown:
   - Moderate cooldown period (approx. 10-15 seconds depending on game balance).

4. Strategic Uses:
   - Escape: Swap with an ally to escape danger.
   - Engage: Swap with an enemy to pull them into a disadvantageous position.
   - Confusion: Disorient enemies by swapping during combat to change camera angles.
   - Teamplay: Coordinate with teammates to confuse opponents or set up combos.

Visual Indicators:
- A distinct visual effect (e.g., flash or particle swirl) appears at both locations.
- The camera smoothly transitions to the new perspective after the swap.

Notes for AI Training:
- Look for sudden changes in player coordinates without intermediate movement frames.
- Detect rapid camera angle shifts coinciding with position changes.
- Identify visual effects associated with the swap animation.
- Track two entities exchanging positions simultaneously.

Example Scenario:
Player A (User) is at (X:100, Y:200). Player B (Target) is at (X:500, Y:600).
After activating R-skill:
- Player A moves instantly to (X:500, Y:600).
- Player B moves instantly to (X:100, Y:200).
- Both players' camera directions adjust to face the new orientation.
